Right Outside Your Door

Lauderdale-by-the-Sea Is a Turtle Town

LBTS's two-mile beach accounts for roughly 8% of all sea turtle nests in Broward County each season — more than 200 nests in an active year. The National Save the Sea Turtle Foundation is headquartered right here in town. If you are staying with us between May and August, you may wake up to fresh turtle tracks in the sand right below your window.
